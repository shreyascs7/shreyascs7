# 🐍 Activating the Contribution Snake Game

To make the animated snake play on your profile, you need to grant the GitHub Action permission to save the created animation.

---

### 🟢 Step 1: Enable Write Permissions

1. Go to your repository on GitHub: **`shreyascs7/shreyascs7`**
2. Click on the **Settings** tab.
3. In the left sidebar, click **Actions** -> **General**.
4. Scroll down to **Workflow permissions**.
5. Select **Read and write permissions**.
6. Click **Save** 💾.

---

### 🔵 Step 2: Trigger the Animation (First Time)

By default, it runs every 12 hours, but you can trigger it now:

1. Click on the **Actions** tab in your repository.
2. In the left sidebar, click **generate animation**.
3. Click the **Run workflow** dropdown on the right.
4. Click **Run workflow** (green button).

---

Once the action finishes running (takes ~30 seconds), your profile will automatically render the snake game eating your contribution green nodes! 🚀
